About ABD Transportation Inc.

Founded in 2019 in Mississauga, Ontario, ABD Transportation Inc. is a trusted name in last-mile delivery services across the Greater Toronto Area (GTA). Our mission is to provide reliable, efficient, and customer-focused delivery solutions tailored to the needs of businesses and individuals.

With a commitment to excellence, we leverage advanced routing technology to ensure your packages reach their destination on time, every time. Based in the heart of Mississauga, we proudly serve the entire GTA with professionalism and care.

Our Services

Last-Mile Delivery

We specialize in last-mile delivery, ensuring your packages are delivered swiftly and securely to their final destination within the GTA. Our team is dedicated to meeting your delivery needs with precision.

Advanced Driver App

Our driver app empowers drivers with tools to capture photos, scan barcodes, collect contactless signatures, verify recipient ages, and add delivery notes, ensuring a seamless delivery experience.

Customizable Notifications

Automate notifications to keep customers informed at key moments, such as when a delivery is dispatched or completed, ensuring timely and relevant updates.

Real-Time Tracking

Our real-time GPS tracking provides accurate, predictive ETAs for customers and complete visibility into driver locations, enhancing delivery efficiency.

Protected Privacy

Masked phone numbers enable direct communication between drivers and customers without compromising privacy, ensuring secure and professional interactions.

Reliable and Efficient Service

Using advanced routing and tracking technology, we optimize delivery routes to provide fast, dependable service while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism.
